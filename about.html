About Nova Search

Our Mission

Nova Search is an independent, open-source search engine built with privacy and transparency at its core. We believe in providing unbiased search results without tracking or storing personal information.

Key Features

Privacy First

No tracking, no personal data collection, no third-party involvement in search results.

Open Source

Our entire codebase is open source and available for review and contributions.

Independent Results

Unbiased search results free from commercial influence.

Fast & Efficient

Optimized for speed and resource efficiency.

Join Our Community

Nova Search is built by the community, for the community. We welcome contributions from developers, researchers, and privacy advocates who share our vision.
