Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

Introduction

At Nova Search, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our search engine.

Information We Don't Collect

  • Personal identification information
  • Search history
  • IP addresses
  • Browser fingerprints
  • Cookies for tracking

Limited Data Collection

We collect minimal anonymous aggregate data solely for:

  • Service maintenance and improvement
  • Prevention of automated abuse
  • Basic system diagnostics

Third-Party Services

Nova Search uses Cloudflare for analytics. We do not use any advertising networks and we don't plan on adding any in the future.

Search Apps

Search Apps may use external services/APIs to provide information. A list of APIs used is below

  • Free Dictionary API - https://dictionaryapi.dev/ - Used by Dictionary Search App.

Your Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request data deletion
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability

However, since we don't collect personal data, these rights mainly serve as an assurance of our privacy-first approach.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
